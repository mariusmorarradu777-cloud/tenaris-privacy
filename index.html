Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Tenaris Silcotub Taxi operates a private internal taxi booking application exclusively for Tenaris Silcotub employees. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Full name and company email (via Microsoft SSO)
  • Personnel number
  • Phone number
  • Pickup and drop-off location addresses
  • Ride scheduling details
  • Cost center code for internal billing
  • Ride history and status updates

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage ride requests
  • To assign drivers and notify relevant parties
  • To send ride confirmations and status updates via email
  • To calculate and split ride costs among participants
  • To generate internal reports for cost center billing

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is only shared within the Tenaris Silcotub organization — with fleet managers, assigned drivers, and ride participants. We do not share data with external third parties.

5. Data Security

All data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption. Access is restricted to authenticated Tenaris Silcotub employees only, using Microsoft SSO.

6. Data Retention

Ride data is retained as long as necessary for internal reporting and billing. You may request deletion by contacting the application administrator.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact your IT department or HR for such requests.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Tenaris Silcotub IT department.
